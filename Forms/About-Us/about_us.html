About Us

An Israeli fashion designer, established in 2010 at “MALIKA”.

Israeli made fashion, which believes in the feminine power and beauty of the different cuts of each one. Carefully designed fashion items with thought of cuts that will enhance the woman’s body and make you feel the best and most comfortable, using quality fabrics and comfortable and elegant designs. Production quality is our top priority out of a belief in smart consumerism and the environment; A fashion item designed in a classic cut, made of quality fabric can accompany you for many seasons to come and not just be a temporary item in the closet. In addition, we believe that you should feel comfort while maintaining a meticulous, elegant and flattering look, and designing collections that can suit women as they feel whole and confident. The combination of fabrics, colors and prints along with the feminine and sophisticated cuts all characterize Neta’s unique design line that is specially designed for women who believe in the power of fashion.
